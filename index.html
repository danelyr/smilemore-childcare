ABOUT US

SmileMore ChildCare is dedicated to creating a safe, loving, and engaging environment where children can learn, play, and grow with confidence. We focus on nurturing each child’s development through hands-on activities, structured routines, caring guidance, and meaningful parent communication. Our goal is to support both children and families by providing dependable care and a joyful early learning experience.

Programs & Daily Activities

A look at how children learn, play, and grow with us

  • Circle Time

    Children begin the day with songs, greetings, and group learning activities that build communication, listening, and social skills.

    Age Group : All Children Focus : Communication & Social Skills Setting : Group Activity Time : Morning Goal : Build confidence and connection

  • Creative Play

    Children explore creativity through drawing, painting, building blocks, puzzles, and imaginative play. These activities help develop fine motor skills and encourage children to express their ideas and imagination.

    Age Group : All children Focus : Creativity & Imagination Activity Type : Hands-on learning Time : Late morning Goal : Encourage creative thinking

  • Music & Movement

    Children enjoy music, dancing, and movement activities that help build coordination, rhythm, and confidence. Songs, instruments, and playful motion keep kids engaged while supporting physical and social development.

    Age Group : Toddlers & Preschool Focus : Movement & Coordination Activity Type : Music, dancing, rhythm Time : Midday Goal : Encourage confidence and active play

  • Outdoor Play

    Outdoor play gives children the opportunity to move, explore, and enjoy fresh air. Activities may include playground time, games, nature exploration, and physical movement that supports healthy development.

    Age Group : All children Focus : Physical Development Activity Type : Outdoor exploration Time : Afternoon Goal : Build strength, coordination, and teamwork Duration : 5days

  • Lunch & Social Time

    Children enjoy lunch together while practicing social skills, conversation, and independence. Mealtime helps build friendships and encourages healthy routines in a relaxed environment.

    Age Group : All children Focus : Social Development Activity Type : Group mealtime Time : Early afternoon Goal : Encourage independence and friendships

  • Quiet Time & Rest

    Quiet time allows children to rest, relax, and recharge. Some children nap while others enjoy calm activities such as reading or quiet play, helping everyone feel refreshed for the rest of the day.

    Age Group : All children Focus : Rest & emotional balance Activity Type : Quiet time / nap Time : Afternoon Goal : Support healthy routines and well-being
